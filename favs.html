This is a log of my favourite films OF ALL TIME!!!

Leave a message on my guestbook telling me your favourite movie


A Real Pain

(Jesse Eisenberg, 2024)

This felt unbelievably relevent and made me want to cry almost through the whole film. It packs a punch in a really tight and concise 89 minute runtime. It contained a heartfelt and empathetic depiction of two emotionally dysfunctional cousins who are foils to eachother. One whose unruly emotion takes over his life, and another who supresses his emotions too much.

Willy Wonka & the Chocolate Factory

(Mel Stuart, 1971)

This movie imprinted on my brain from my childhood, it is one of the most charming musicals of all time.

The Matrix

(Lana Wachowski, Lilly Wachowski, 1999)

Mindblowing film made in Sydney! I've visited a couple of the filming locations which is cool. It still holds up to today and the message just gets more and more important as tech develops.
