San Francisco Reflection: 3 full days

-Simon Owen

I figure California is one of the most beautiful states in the U.S., and that San Francisco is its most beautiful city.

The transportation was one of the main attractions: streetcars, cable cars, trolleys, and Waymos.

Streetcars: colourful streamline tram-like vehicles from the 30s and 40s, collected from all over the world and transported to act as a living transport museum integrated as practical public transport in San Francisco.

Cable cars: brown old-fashioned behicles pulled by a system of cables just below the surface of the road. They started in 1893 making them over 150 years old.

Trolleys: electric buses with tram-like arms connecting to over-wire power lines.

Waymo: Google's self-driving service to replace Uber, currently operates in Phoenix, San Francisco Bay Area, Los Angeles, Austin, and Atlanta.
